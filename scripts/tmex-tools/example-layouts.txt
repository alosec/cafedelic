# Tmex Layout Pattern Examples

## Basic Patterns

113     - Three columns: 1 | 1 | 3 panes
122     - Three columns: 1 | 2 | 2 panes  
131     - Three columns: 1 | 3 | 1 panes
1111    - Four equal columns
22      - Two columns with 2 panes each
33      - Two columns with 3 panes each

## Nested Patterns

1[123]1   - Three columns: 1 | [1,2,3 rows] | 1
[12][34]  - Two columns with different row counts
1[22]3    - Three columns with nested middle section

## Size-Adjusted Patterns (using {})

2{1,3}2   - Three columns where middle column has 1:3 ratio
3{1,1,2}  - Three panes with size ratio 1:1:2
4{1,1,1,2} - Four panes where last is twice as big

## Transpose Flag

Add -t or --transpose to any pattern for horizontal layout:
113 -t    - Three rows instead of columns
122 -t    - Horizontal stack of 1, 2, 2 panes

## Cafedelic-Specific Layouts

1[123]1     - Activity | [Tree, Editor, Terminal] | Context
131 -t      - Horizontal: Monitor, 3 work panes, logs
2{1,3}[12]  - Left sidebar, main area with sub-panes
[12]3{1,2}  - Complex layout with sized regions

## Tips

- Numbers represent pane counts in each section
- [] create nested layouts within sections  
- {} adjust relative sizes of panes
- Combine patterns for complex layouts
- Use transpose for horizontal arrangements
